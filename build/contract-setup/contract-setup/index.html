    Editing Contract Details#

    1. In the module menu, click Projects.
      The PROJECTS list page is displayed.
    2. In the PROJECTS list page, click the required project, and then click Edit.
    3. In the navigation pane, expand the project folder, and then expand the Contracts folder.
    4. In the navigation pane, click the required contract folder.
    5. Click Edit.
      The EDIT CONTRACT page is displayed.
      Note:

      • You can edit the measurement system only if contract items are not added in the CONTRACT ITEMS page.

        For information on Contract Items, refer to Managing Contract Items.

      • You cannot update the contract calendar if contract items are locked in the contract.

        For information on contract items, refer to Managing Contract Items. For information on selecting a contract calendar, refer to Creating a Contract.

    6. Update the required fields, and then click Save.
